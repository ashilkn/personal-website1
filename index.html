I have always enjoyed learning tech and doing it, I love to learn. Learing is something that I didn't get in college so that's the reason why I dropped out of college during my final year. I just couldn't waste 6 more months staying there. I had already found my passion which is music and tech and college was the only thing that was holding me back from working hard towards my goals. So yeah, I dropped out!

I have been studying guitar by myself for the last 9 years. I was so passionate about it that I worked really hard that I started getting first place in school every year after two years of learing guitar. I have gotten 2nd place in my district and I remember that feeling when I realised that I'm the 2nd best from all the CBSE schools in the distirct. From then I started going for live shows, started teaching, doing recordings and stuff!

Right now in my life, I have taken a break from music. I'm currently concentrating on learning tech. I have been interested in coding since highschool. I love problem solving, I love the dopamine rush after I solve a problem be it math, physics or computer programs. I am fortunate to have found a good place where I can learn and grow which is SPS by Crossroads Academy. Now I'm learing something new in tech and many other essential things everyday!
